Monthly Photo Contest

Welcome to the CAMshots monthly photo contest! Each month, we invite photographers of all levels to submit their best work in various categories. Winning entries are showcased on our website and celebrated for their creativity and technical skill.

How to Enter

Participating in the CAMshots photo contest is simple. Follow these steps to submit your photos:

  1. Choose your category: Each month we have different categories such as Child Photos, Flower Photos, Scenic Photos, etc.
  2. Email your entry: Send your photo in JPEG format to gerry@camshots.com.
  3. Include your details: Make sure to include your name, the photo category, and the camera settings used for the shot.

Submission Guidelines

To ensure fairness and quality, please adhere to the following guidelines when submitting your photos:

  • Photos must be in JPEG format.
  • Maximum file size is 10MB.
  • Only one entry per category per month.
  • Photos should be original and taken by the entrant.
  • No watermarks or signatures on the photos.
